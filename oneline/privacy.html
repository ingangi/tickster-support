OneLine

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026 · Effective from first use

Short version: OneLine does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All reading data and settings are stored locally on your device. The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising. No account registration is required.

1. Who We Are

OneLine is developed by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). The app is distributed through the iOS App Store.

Contact: enuaui@163.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

When you use OneLine, you may create or import content stored only on your device:

  • Imported book text (via camera OCR, photos, TXT/EPUB, or manual input)
  • Reading progress and history
  • Bookmarks and notes
  • Comprehension check responses
  • Custom reward configurations set by parents

This data is created and controlled entirely by you.

2.2 Automatically Generated Data

OneLine stores the following on your device:

  • Reading statistics (time, books completed, streaks)
  • Achievement progress and unlock history
  • Reward redemption records
  • User preferences (theme, font size, TTS voice)

2.3 What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No browsing history or usage analytics
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No account registration

3. How Data Is Stored

StorageWhat's StoredAccessible By
SwiftDataBooks, history, bookmarks, achievements, rewardsYou only
UserDefaultsPreferences, theme, settingsYou only

All data is stored locally within the app's sandbox on your iOS device. No data is transmitted to any server. No network communication occurs at any point.

4. Device Permissions

OneLine may request the following permissions. All are optional:

PermissionPurposeRequired
CameraScan printed text via OCRNo
Photo LibrarySelect images for text recognitionNo
MicrophoneVoice input for comprehension checksNo
Speech RecognitionProcess spoken responsesNo

Speech recognition is performed on-device using Apple's Speech framework. No audio is sent to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

OneLine uses only Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • SwiftUI — user interface
  • SwiftData — local data persistence
  • AVFoundation — text-to-speech playback
  • Speech — on-device speech recognition
  • Vision — on-device OCR
  • PhotosUI — photo picker

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media SDKs.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone:

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data
  • We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not share data with any third-party services
  • No data is transmitted over the internet at any time

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • All data is retained on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling OneLine permanently removes all local data
  • You can delete individual books, history, bookmarks, and rewards at any time

If you want all data deleted, uninstall the app from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

OneLine is designed for children ages 8 to 15, including those with attention difficulties such as ADHD:

  • No account registration — no way for children to share personal information
  • No data is collected, transmitted, or accessible outside the device
  • Complies with COPPA — no personal information is collected from children
  • Parent-configurable rewards and a hidden parent dashboard give parents full control
  • No in-app purchases, no advertising, no external links

9. Security

OneLine runs within Apple's iOS App Sandbox, which strictly limits access to system resources. Since no data leaves the device, the attack surface is minimal. All text recognition and speech processing is performed on-device.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control:

  • Access: All data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete any item at any time; uninstall to remove all data
  • No lock-in: No account exists, so there is no data held by us

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
