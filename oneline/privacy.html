ONELINE

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026 · Effective from first use

TL;DR: OneLine does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All reading data, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device. The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising. No account registration is required.

1. WHO WE ARE

OneLine is developed by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). Our app is distributed through the iOS App Store.

Contact email: enuaui@163.com

2. DATA WE COLLECT

2.1 Data You Provide

When you use OneLine, you may create or import content that is stored on your device:

  • Imported book text (via camera OCR, photo library, TXT/EPUB files, or manual input)
  • Reading progress and history
  • Bookmarks and notes
  • Comprehension check responses (text or voice input)
  • Custom reward configurations set by parents

This data is created and controlled entirely by you, stored only on your device.

2.2 Automatically Generated Data

OneLine stores the following automatically on your device:

  • Reading statistics (total reading time, books completed, streaks)
  • Achievement progress and unlock history
  • Reward redemption records
  • User preferences (theme, font size, TTS voice selection)

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • No location data
  • No browsing history or usage analytics
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No account registration of any kind

3. HOW DATA IS STORED

StorageWhat's StoredAccessible By
SwiftData (Local)Books, reading history, bookmarks, achievements, rewardsYou only (on-device)
UserDefaultsPreferences, theme, font size, settingsYou only (on-device)

All data is stored locally within the app's sandbox on your iOS device. No data is transmitted to any server. No network communication occurs at any point.

4. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

OneLine may request the following device permissions. All are optional and only used when you actively enable the corresponding feature:

PermissionPurposeRequired?
CameraScan printed text via OCR for importNo — only for camera import
Photo LibrarySelect images for text recognitionNo — only for photo import
MicrophoneVoice input during comprehension checksNo — typing is always available
Speech RecognitionProcess spoken responses for comprehensionNo — only for voice input

Speech recognition processing is performed on-device using Apple's built-in Speech framework. No audio is sent to any server.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

OneLine uses only Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • SwiftUI — user interface
  • SwiftData — local data persistence
  • AVFoundation / AVFAudio — text-to-speech audio playback
  • Speech — on-device speech recognition
  • Vision — on-device OCR text recognition
  • PhotosUI — photo picker for image selection
  • NaturalLanguage — text processing

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media SDKs. No third-party code is bundled with the app.

6. DATA SHARING

We do not share your data with anyone. Specifically:

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data
  • We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not share data with any third-party services
  • No data is transmitted over the internet at any time

7. DATA RETENTION & DELETION

  • All data is retained on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling OneLine permanently removes all local data — there is no backup or recovery
  • You can delete individual books, reading history, bookmarks, and rewards at any time from within the app

If you want all your data deleted, simply uninstall the app from your device.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

OneLine is designed for children ages 8–15, including those with attention difficulties such as ADHD. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • No account registration is required — there is no way for children to share personal information with us
  • No data is collected, transmitted, or accessible to anyone outside the device
  • The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) as no personal information is collected from children
  • Parent-configurable rewards and the hidden parent dashboard give parents full control over the experience
  • No in-app purchases, no advertising, no external links accessible to children

9. SECURITY

OneLine runs within Apple's iOS App Sandbox, which strictly limits access to system resources and other apps' data. Since no data leaves the device, the attack surface is minimal. All text recognition and speech processing is performed on-device.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Since OneLine stores all data locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete any book, history entry, or bookmark at any time; uninstall to remove all data
  • No lock-in: Since no account exists, there is no data held by us to request

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
