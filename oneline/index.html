About

OneLine is a reading training app designed for children ages 8 to 15, especially those who find it hard to stay focused while reading. By showing one line at a time, it cuts visual distractions and builds real reading habits — with text-to-speech narration, comprehension checks, and a reward system to keep kids motivated.

Setup

Permissions

OneLine may ask for the following permissions. All are optional and only used when you actively enable the feature:

  • Camera — scan printed text via OCR
  • Photo Library — select images for text recognition
  • Microphone — voice input during comprehension checks
  • Speech Recognition — process spoken responses

If you denied a permission, you can enable it in Settings → OneLine.

Parent Dashboard

Press and hold the header area for 3 seconds to access the hidden parent dashboard. From here you can configure rewards, view reading statistics, and manage achievement settings.

Customization

  • Three themes: Light, Sepia, Dark
  • Four font size levels
  • Adjustable line spacing
  • TTS voice: female, male, child

Troubleshooting

  • Camera OCR not working: Make sure Camera permission is granted in Settings → OneLine. Use good lighting and keep the text in focus.
  • Text-to-Speech not playing: Check that your device isn't in silent mode. Go to Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content to verify Chinese voices are downloaded.
  • Voice input not responding: Make sure Microphone permission is granted. Speak clearly in a quiet environment.
  • Data lost after update: All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all reading data.
