专注阅读 — iOS Reading Training App

ABOUT

OneLine (一行) is a reading training app designed for children with attention difficulties (ages 8–15). By displaying one line at a time, it reduces visual distraction and helps build focused reading habits — combining text-to-speech narration, comprehension checks, and a reward system to keep children motivated.

SETUP

Permissions

OneLine may ask for the following permissions. All are optional and only used when you actively enable the feature:

  • Camera — for scanning printed text via OCR
  • Photo Library — for selecting images to recognize text from
  • Microphone — for voice input during comprehension checks
  • Speech Recognition — for processing your spoken responses

If you denied a permission, you can enable it in Settings → OneLine on your iOS device.

Parent Dashboard

Parents can access a hidden dashboard by pressing and holding the header area for 3 seconds. From here you can configure rewards, view reading statistics, and manage achievement settings.

Customization

  • Three reading themes: Light, Sepia, Dark
  • Four font size levels
  • Adjustable line spacing
  • TTS voice options: female, male, child

TROUBLESHOOTING

  • Camera OCR not working: Ensure camera permission is granted in Settings → OneLine. Make sure the text is well-lit and in focus.
  • Text-to-Speech not playing: Check that your device is not in silent mode. Go to Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content to verify Chinese voices are downloaded.
  • Voice input not responding: Ensure microphone permission is granted. Speak clearly in a quiet environment.
  • Data lost after app update: All data is stored locally on your device. Make sure the app is not uninstalled, as that will permanently remove all reading data.
