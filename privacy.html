TICKSTER

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026 · Effective from first use

TL;DR: Tickster does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. All task data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync (Pro feature), your encrypted data is stored in your own iCloud account via CloudKit private database, which even Apple cannot read.

1. WHO WE ARE

Tickster is developed by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). Our app is distributed through the Mac App Store.

Contact email: enuaui@163.com

2. DATA WE COLLECT

2.1 Data You Provide

When you use Tickster, you may create tasks that contain:

  • Task titles and descriptions (text you enter)
  • Schedule preferences (times, days, recurrence rules)
  • Exclusion rules (dates or time ranges to skip)

This data is created and controlled entirely by you.

2.2 Automatically Generated Data

Tickster stores the following automatically on your device:

  • Notification delivery history (last 10 records per task, for displaying in-app)
  • Purchase receipt for Pro unlock (stored via App Group shared storage)
  • Sync timestamps (if iCloud sync is enabled)

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • No location data
  • No browsing history or usage analytics
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No cookies or tracking pixels

3. HOW DATA IS STORED

StorageWhat's StoredAccessible By
Local UserDefaultsTasks, notification history, preferencesYou only (on-device)
App Group ContainerWidget data, purchase statusTickster app & its extensions
CloudKit Private DBTasks (if iCloud sync enabled)You only (via your Apple ID)

All local data is stored in plain JSON format within your macOS user profile. No encryption is applied locally because the data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync.

4. ICLOUD SYNC (PRO FEATURE)

If you purchase Tickster Pro and enable iCloud sync:

  • Your task data is synced via Apple CloudKit Private Database
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Only you can access this data — neither we nor Apple can read it
  • Sync is optional and can be disabled at any time from the app menu
  • Disabling sync stops all CloudKit access but does not delete previously synced data from your iCloud account (you can manage this in System Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage)

5. NOTIFICATIONS

Tickster sends local notifications only to remind you of your scheduled tasks. These are delivered through macOS's built-in notification system (UserNotifications framework).

  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  • No push notification server is used
  • No notification content is sent to any server
  • You can disable notifications at any time in System Settings → Notifications

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Tickster uses only Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • StoreKit 2 — for in-app purchase processing (handled by Apple)
  • CloudKit — for optional iCloud sync (handled by Apple)
  • WidgetKit — for desktop widgets
  • UserNotifications — for local notifications

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media SDKs.

7. DATA SHARING

We do not share your data with anyone. Specifically:

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data
  • We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not share data with any third-party services
  • Apple may process your in-app purchase as part of App Store operations, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

8. DATA RETENTION & DELETION

  • Task data is retained until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling Tickster removes all local data
  • iCloud-synced data can be removed by deleting tasks in the app, or by managing iCloud storage in System Settings
  • Notification history is limited to the 10 most recent records per task and cycles automatically

If you want all your data deleted, simply delete all tasks within the app and disable iCloud sync.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Tickster does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is a general-purpose productivity tool and does not target any specific age group. If you believe a child has provided personal data through our app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

10. SECURITY

Tickster runs within Apple's App Sandbox, which limits access to system resources. All iCloud data uses CloudKit's private database with encryption provided by Apple. While no system is perfectly secure, we follow Apple's best practices for data protection.

11. YOUR RIGHTS

Since Tickster stores all data locally or in your personal iCloud account, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete any task at any time; uninstall to remove all data
  • Portability: Task data is stored in standard JSON format
  • Withdrawal of consent: Disable notifications or iCloud sync at any time in System Settings

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
